The Nicomachean Ethics
is based on the lectures of
Aristotle
and was compiled around 340 BC.
It was translated from Greek in 1906 by
F. H. Peters.

This ebook was produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
David M. Gross,
and is based on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
The School of Athens,
a painting completed in 1511 by
Raphael.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
